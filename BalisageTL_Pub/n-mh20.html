Balisage: The Markup Conference

<quote>

Inline Quote

Any text to be displayed in quotes for reasons of argument or rhetoric, or in order to refer to a term or phrase as such (i.e., as “mentioned”). This may or may not be an explicit quotation.

Expanded Content Model

(#PCDATA | inlinemediaobject | superscript | subscript | xref | link | emphasis | footnote | trademark | citation | code)*

Description

Any combination of:

  • Text, numbers, or special characters
  • <inlinemediaobject> Inline Media Object
  • <superscript> Superscript
  • <subscript> Subscript
  • <xref> Cross-Reference
  • <link> Link
  • <emphasis> Emphasis
  • <footnote> Footnote
  • <trademark> Trademark
  • <citation> Citation Reference
  • <code> Code

This element may be contained in:

<bibliomixed>, <citation>, <emphasis>, <link>, <para>, <subtitle>, <td>, <term>, <th>, <title>
