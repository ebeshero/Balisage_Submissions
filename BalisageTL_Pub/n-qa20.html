<legalnotice>

Legal Notice

Statement of copyright date and ownership and licensing for the conference paper, in presentation form.

Remarks

This element is available only in the production DTD; it will be provided to papers published in conference proceedings during editing.

Papers submitted to Balisage are presumed to be copyrighted by their authors, and will be shared within the conference committee and among peer reviewers on that basis.

When a paper is accepted for Balisage, its authors are asked to sign a publication agreement, stating the terms under which they license the paper for publication in the conference proceedings. This element is then added to the paper using the language provided by the authors.
